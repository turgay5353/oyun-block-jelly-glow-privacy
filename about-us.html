Block Jelly Glow

About Us

Block Jelly Glow is a free, glowing block-puzzle game: drag colorful jelly blocks onto the board, fill rows and columns to clear them, and chain combos for satisfying, sparkling explosions.

Play your way across two modes:

  • Classic — endless play. Beat your own high score, build big combos, and climb as far as you can.
  • Adventure — handcrafted levels with target scores and special Gem Quest challenges to collect.

We're a small, independent team that loves clean, juicy, easy-to-pick-up puzzle games. Block Jelly Glow is built with care for smooth, satisfying play on mobile.
